About

What?

The Scoree application shows the examination results in the simplest form. This application currently covers the results of BCA and MCA courses of Indira Gandhi National Open University with the first series of release.
The Scoree is the first initiative by the Codolic coding group.

Why are we here?

In simple words, to make it easy and convenient for the students to check their results, thereby saving their precious time.

How?

Follow these 3 easy steps to use it:
  1. Select the programme you are enrolled in
  2. Enter your enrollment number
  3. Hit Enter/Go
